Death Star Design Initiation Meeting

Meeting Topic: Design Kick-Off

Date: Galactic Standard Date 4012.04.16

Click to reveal more about the Death Star project

The Death Star, an armored space station with enough power to destroy an entire planet, is the Galactic Empire's ultimate weapon. Its construction is overseen by the Empire's elite engineers and scientists.

This project aims to ensure the dominance of the Empire over the galaxy. However, it has a critical vulnerability that the Rebel Alliance will seek to exploit. This vulnerability must be addressed in the next phases of development.
